About Bannari Amman Institute of Technology

Bannari Amman Institute of Technology (BIT) is an autonomous engineering college located in Sathyamangalam, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1996 by the Bannari Amman Group, BIT is renowned for its academic excellence and industry-oriented curriculum.

History of BIT

The institute was founded with the vision to provide quality technical education and contribute to the development of skilled professionals in various engineering disciplines. Over the years, BIT has grown into one of the premier technical institutions in the region, known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure and commitment to excellence.

Academic Excellence

BIT offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. The institute emphasizes practical learning and industry exposure to prepare students for the challenges of the modern world.

Campus Facilities

The sprawling campus of BIT is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern laboratories, libraries, and sports infrastructure. The college also provides hostel accommodation and transportation facilities for the convenience of students.
