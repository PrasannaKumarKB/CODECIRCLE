About The Campus

Bannari Amman Institute of Technology is an Autonomous, Self-financing Engineering College, Approved by AICTE, New Delhi and Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. Nestled on the banks of The River Bhavani, BIT campus provides environment for learning in harmony with nature, away from the odds of the city life. The spacious and the earth hugging buildings punctuated with landscaped courtyards and pathways are designed to emphasise the business ethics and or characteristics of an excellent centre for learning. The Campus hosts well planned academic blocks, computer centres, lecture halls, libraries, laboratories, conference halls, staff quarters, hostels and students' centres. The Campus also houses a co-operative store, ATM (Axis, SBI and KVB) and a clinic to attend to the general health of the students and staff.

Special Labs

Gurugulam Skills

Day & Night Skills

BIT Special Laboratories provide a platform for students to learn, practice, and innovate on the globally sought after skills, beside product development. BIT Special Laboratories established over-and-over the regular labs mandated by the University / AICTE, trains students to participate in internationally reputed competitions.

The benchmarking initiative of BIT Gurugulam was established from the year 2021. The Gurugulam provides students with hands-on experience in foundation skills under faculty supervision. Through the program, students will get acquainted with professional and technical skills.

Our college offers a comprehensive array of day and night skills programs to cater to the diverse needs of our student community. By day, students can immerse themselves in workshops and seminars designed to complement their academic curriculum, fostering practical skills vital for personal and professional growth.

Learning Centre

Vedanayagam Auditorium

Research Centre

A centrally air - conditioned, five storyed new Learning Centre was built in an area of 8240 sq.m (88662 sq.ft) on par with international standards with a view to cater to the prerequisites of pedagogy in an exemplary manner. The fully automated Learning Centre provides an ambience conducive for the teaching and learning process with the support of 96,000 volumes of text and reference books with RFID based Library Management System.

Within the vibrant campus of our college, we are proud to boast two exceptional auditoriums: the BIT Auditorium and the Vedhanayagam Auditorium. The BIT Auditorium, with its impressive capacity of 1800 seats, stands as a hallmark of grandeur and sophistication. It serves as a premier venue for hosting large-scale events, conferences, cultural extravaganzas, and academic ceremonies, providing a spacious and captivating environment for attendees.

Nestled within our college campus lies a beacon of innovation: our state-of-the-art Research Park. This cutting-edge facility is equipped with advanced machinery and cutting-edge technology, providing a dynamic environment for groundbreaking research and development. From precision instruments to cutting-edge equipment, our Research Park offers researchers and students unparalleled resources to explore, experiment, and innovate across various disciplines.
